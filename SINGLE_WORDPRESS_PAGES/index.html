The Registered Massage Therapists’ Association of Ontario has partnered with ClinicSense (based in Toronto) to provide RMTAO members a modern practice management software system at discounted rates.

ClinicSense helps eliminate paperwork, so you can stay organized and have more time to treat clients and do the things that you love. Additionally, their email marketing tools help you attract new clients and have them rebook often.

The incredibly easy-to-use software offers:

  • ONLINE SOAP NOTES

    With clinicSense you type your appointment notes directly into the client record for quick and easy accessibility in the future.

  • ONLINE INTAKE FORMS

    ClinicSense emails a secure, online intake form to new clients to fill out before coming to your office, and uploads it to your client records.

  • APPOINTMENT SCHEDULING

    Accessible from everywhere, your online calendar adds convenience when you're out of the clinic. Plus, your clients can book their own appointment online.

  • TAKE PAYMENTS

    Securely charge credit cards directly in ClinicSense. Optionally ask clients to prepay or just save their credit card number when they book online.

  • SELL GIFT CERTIFICATES

    Sell, manage, & promote gift certificates sales with ClinicSense. Add the "Buy Gift Certificate" button to your website to drive online sales.

  • EMAIL & TEXT REMINDERS

    ClinicSense automatically sends out appointment reminders. It's totally hands off for you and really helps reduce no-shows.

  • EMAIL MARKETING

    Use ClinicSense to send email campaigns to your clients. Optionally include a promo code to boost sales.

  • BIRTHDAY PROMO

    ClinicSense can automatically email your clients on their birthday. Optionally include a birthday promotion like $10 off their next treatment.
